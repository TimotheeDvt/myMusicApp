24-EDO (Quarter-Tones)

Tone Sizes

24-EDO introduces three new tone sizes beyond the semitone (100c) and whole tone (200c).

  • Quarter Tone (50c): Smallest size. Highly dissonant, "beating" sound.
  • Neutral Tone (150c): Between a semitone and whole tone. Used in Arabic and Persian music.
  • Wide Tone (250c): A metallic, "moody" whole tone.

24-EDO Interval Categories

  • Neutral Third (350c): Approx. 11/9. Right between major and minor.
  • Wide Fourth (550c): Approx. 11/8 (11th harmonic).
  • Narrow Fifth (650c): Approx. 16/11. Often called the "Wolf Fifth".

Neutral Triads

Built using neutral thirds (350c).

  • Neutral Triad Cneu 0 - 7 - 14
    (C - Ed - G)
  • Neutral Tetrad Cneu 0 - 7 - 14 - 21
    (C - Ed - G - B)

Triad Alterations

  • Ultra Triad (Upmajor): 0 - 9 - 14 (uses Ultra Third 450c).
  • Infra Triad (Downminor): 0 - 5 - 14 (uses Infra Third 250c).

Harmonic Approximations

24-EDO is excellent at approximating the 11th harmonic (11/8).

Super-extended Major: 4:5:6:9:11:13 structure.

Traditional Microtonal Scales

  • Maqam Rast: 4 - 3 - 3 - 4 - 4 - 3 - 3 (steps in 24-edo).
  • Maqam Bayati: 3 - 3 - 4 - 4 - 2 - 1 - 3 - 4.
  • Maqam Saba: 3 - 3 - 2 - 6 - 2 - 4 - 4.

New Pentatonics

  • Godzilla: 0 - 5 - 10 - 14 - 19 - 24 (Equi-pentatonic approx.).
  • Mohajira: 0 - 4 - 7 - 11 - 14 - 18 - 21 - 24 (Arabic-style heptatonic).
